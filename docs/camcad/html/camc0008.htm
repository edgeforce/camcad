$# About CAMCAD Modules & Editions

CAMCAD is organized in various editions, which are purchased according to your needs:

CAMCAD Shareware: At this level, CAMCAD acts as a viewer only. Supported formats are DXF, HPGL/HPGL2, Gerber/Gerber-X, and IPC350/356/356a.

CAMCAD Graphic: This edition of CAMCAD specializes in the reading/writing of graphical CAD/CAM data. Formats such as DXF, Gerber, HPGL, IGES, and IPC are supported, with full editing capability. Over 35 modules in total.

CAMCAD Professional: For users who need to retain complete intelligence in their PCB designs upon import into CAMCAD, we offer the Professional Edition. All attributes are retained, including netlist and part information, for full data capturing/data mining. Manufacturing(.ccm) and Bill Of Materials(.bom) outputs found in this edition; over 55 modules possible.

CAMCAD 3D-ECAD: Create 3D solid models of PCB designs for electromechanical packaging in SolidWorks97. Component heights are extracted automatically from the CAD databases and user setup files. Among the 18 possible modules are readers for PADS, PCAD, ACCEL, REDAC, VERIBEST, MENTOR, CADENCE, PROTEL, and OrCAD.

CAMCAD PCB Translator: CAMCAD functions as a complete PCB migration tool in this edition, with complete retention of all PCB design intelligence. For example, quick and accurate PADS (.asc) to Mentor Board Station V8. Many modules in development - call for details.

CAMCAD HP3070: Read PCB data and export a board file and board XY file for use on the HP3070 machine. Over 18 possible modules.
