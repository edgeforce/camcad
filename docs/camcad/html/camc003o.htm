#Poly Query

The Poly Query window, the negative checkbox, and the Edit Poly Item button

Shows the polyline entry and the vertices of the polyline. The polyline entry may be written as "Poly," "Poly – FILLED," or "Poly - FILLED CLOSED."

1.Move the mouse pointer over the entry (the word Poly) and click to highlight it.

2.Click Edit Poly Item. The Edit Poly dialog box displays with checkboxes for Filled, Closed, and Void Poly.

3.If you wish to edit the polyline, you can toggle the checkboxes that you are interested in , then click OK.

The Edit Poly dialog

The vertices of the polyline can also be highlighted and edited.

1.Move the mouse pointer over a vertex entry and click to highlight it.

2.Click Edit Poly Item. The Edit Poly Vertex/Bulge dialog box displays with the specific attributes of the vertex in question.

If the vertex is an endpoint on a line segment that contains a bulge (for example, an Arc), you will see entries for:

•Bulge Number: The tangent of the angle of the chord distance over half the distance between the two points. Positive and negative values are determined by draw order, and which way the arc is drawn.

•Bulge Radius: The radius of the circle that matches the given arc.

•Bulge Chord Distance: The value from the top of the arc down to a straight line connecting the two endpoints of the arc.

Otherwise, these entries will be 0.000.
