$#Add > Text

Inserts text into your project window.

1.Select Add > Text. A small dialog box with a window for your text appears.

2.Type something in the window. The dialog box does not go away, but if you move your mouse at this point, you will see your text attached to the mouse pointer.

3.Move and position the text to the place you want it to appear, and click the mouse button. The text is placed and the Add > Text dialog disappears.
