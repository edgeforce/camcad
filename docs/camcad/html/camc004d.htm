#Insert Query

X & Y Placement, Scale & Angle Fields, Mirror Checkbox

•X and Y fields: Shows the coordinates of the insertion point for the selected entity. You can adjust these values by clicking in the appropriate field and typing in the new value.

•Scale: Shows the scale at which the entity is displayed. You can modify the scale by clicking in the field and typing in the new value.

•Angle: Refers to the angle in which the geometry is drawn. You can modify the angle by clicking in the field and typing in the new value.

•Mirror: When toggled, mirrors the component about its insertion point.
