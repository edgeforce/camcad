$#Edit > Merge Polyline

Lets you select multiple line segments or polylines and merge them into one large polyline.

1.Select Edit > Merge Polyline to enter "edit" mode.

2.Use the mouse to select each individual line segment that you wish to merge.

3.Select Edit > Merge Polyline again or press [ESC] to get out of "edit" mode.

The selected lines are now merged into one polyline.

Note

You cannot merge lines or polylines from different layers into one polyline.
