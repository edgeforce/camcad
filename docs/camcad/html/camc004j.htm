$# Toolbars

You can toggle CAMCAD toolbars on or off by the selecting View > Toolbars. Toolbars may be repositioned on your screen by dragging with the mouse. Float the mouse pointer over any button on any toolbar to display the button name.

Click on any of the toolbars below for more specific toolbar information.
