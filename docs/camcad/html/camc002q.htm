$#View > Poly Toolbar

Toggles the Poly Toolbar on and off.

All CAMCAD toolbars can be repositioned on the screen.

1.Click and hold the mouse button while the mouse pointer is over an empty section on the toolbar.

2.Drag the toolbar to the new position and release the mouse button. The toolbar is repositioned.
